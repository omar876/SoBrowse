SoBrowse

Socially shop, or browse the shopping sites with friends!

Our mission is making shopping online a social experience, that’s core idea behind SoBrowse. Sobrowse is the only App that connects people that are on the same website to socialize or chat with in real time. with SoBrowse you can invite your friends, or love ones to join you on shopping websites your browsing; to socialize in real time, chat about the content/product on the webpage, share your opinions about a product, or service with friends or other website visitors in real time.


Sobrowse 3 main feathers:

  • Live Open Feed
    • Follow any user from the feed, or create your own Live Follow.
  • Private Group
    • Create a private follow and invite friends.
  • Web Hangouts
    • Hangout on shopping website, and chat with people that are currently browsing the same website.

Going shopping online? invite your friends to shop along with you only on SoBrowseApp.
